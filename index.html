Title: Accident Alert System

Description: Road accidents rate are very high nowadays, One major factor that leads to these accidents becoming fatal is response of the emergency services which in most cases are not notified sufficiently within time. Timely medical aid can help in saving lives. This system aims to alert the family members, friends about the accident to provide immediate medical aid. The attached ACCELEROMETER in the vehicle senses the tilt of the vehicle and vibrations. Thus the system will make the decision and sends the information to the Smartphone as SMS by using GSM MODULE, which contains a GOOGLE MAP Link to provide exact location obtained by GPS MODULE. This whole system is controlled by ARDUINO.
